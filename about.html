01

A problem we saw
no one was solving.

Northwest Washington is home to some of the Pacific Northwest's most beautiful properties — waterfront estates on the San Juan Islands, bluff homes overlooking Puget Sound, custom builds tucked into the Skagit Valley foothills. And nearly every owner of these properties shares the same quiet frustration: there is no one to trust with them.

The options available were a fragmented patchwork: a handyman here, a landscaper there, a property manager who specialized in rentals. No one who treated the home as a whole. No one accountable for its condition. No one who knew it the way its owner did — or better.

Praesidio was built to be that person. We started with a simple model: a flat monthly membership, a vetted network of the best tradespeople in the region, and a commitment to knowing every member's home as thoroughly as they do. We don't manage rental units. We don't flip houses. We care for homes — and we take that responsibility personally.

What we believe

Five principles that
guide every decision
we make for you.

These are not marketing statements. They are operating constraints — the rules we hold ourselves to even when they are inconvenient, and the reason our members trust us with their most valuable assets.

01

Transparency above all

We will never spend a dollar of your money without your knowledge. We will never mark up a vendor invoice. We will never recommend a service you don't need. If something goes wrong, you will hear about it from us — before you notice it yourself.

02

Proactive, not reactive

A roof that leaks is expensive. A roof we caught before it leaked is a $400 repair. We are not a repair service you call after something breaks. We are the reason things don't break in the first place — and when they do, we're already there.

03

Your home knows us

We maintain detailed records of every system, every vendor, every repair, every inspection for every property we manage. When you call us about your boiler, we already know when it was last serviced, who serviced it, and what it's due for next.

04

Only the best tradespeople

We don't use contractors we wouldn't put in our own homes. Every vendor in the Praesidio network has been licensed-verified, insured, background-checked, and personally evaluated. We maintain these relationships actively — and remove vendors who don't meet our standard.

05

Fewer clients, deeper care

We deliberately limit the number of properties we accept. This is not a scalability play — it is a quality commitment. Every member deserves a team that genuinely knows their home. When we are at capacity, we are at capacity.

Our members

Built for homeowners
who expect more.

Praesidio is not for everyone — and we mean that honestly. We are for the homeowner who values quality of care over price, who wants to be able to trust completely, and who understands that exceptional service has a cost worth paying.

Member profile

The full-time primary resident

You own a home valued at $700K or more in Whatcom or Skagit County. You travel frequently for work, have a demanding schedule, or simply want your home maintained to a standard that requires professional oversight. You've had bad experiences with contractors who don't show up, don't perform, or overcharge. You want one number to call and one person who knows your home.

Typically starts with Essential or Premier. Values the tradesman network and the 24-hour response guarantee above all else.

Member profile

The vacation & second homeowner

Your property on Orcas Island, Whidbey, or Camano sits empty for 8–10 months of the year. You arrive to find burst pipes, pest damage, or deferred maintenance that's turned into a $15,000 bill. You want someone who watches your property year-round, prepares it for your arrival, and closes it down properly when you leave — without requiring your presence to manage any of it.

The highest-value segment for Praesidio. Typically Premier or Estate. The ROI on professional management versus emergency repair costs is immediate and obvious.

Member profile

The estate & multi-property owner

You own multiple properties — a primary Bellingham residence, a San Juan Islands retreat, perhaps a family property in Anacortes. The complexity of managing multiple homes across multiple counties, each with its own vendors, systems, and seasonal needs, has become genuinely unmanageable without professional help. You want a single, trusted partner across all of them.

Estate membership, custom-scoped. One house manager, consolidated reporting, unified vendor network. This is the member for whom Praesidio becomes a genuine operating partner.

Member profile

The out-of-state inheritor

You inherited or purchased a property in Northwest Washington but live in California, Texas, or elsewhere. You cannot be on-site to manage it, but you cannot afford to let it deteriorate. You need a trusted local partner who acts as your eyes, your voice, and your decision-maker on the ground — someone accountable enough that you can genuinely stop worrying.

Frequently starts with Premier. The peace of mind factor is the primary driver — the ability to hand over responsibility completely and trust it will be honored.

How we work

From first meeting
to lifetime stewardship.

Every Praesidio relationship begins with a genuine commitment to learn your home — and that learning never stops.

I

Consultation

We meet at your property. We walk every room, every system, every exterior element. We listen to your concerns and your priorities. No obligation, no fee — just a genuine conversation about what your home needs.

II

Assessment

Our team conducts a thorough baseline documentation of your property — condition, systems, deferred maintenance, and seasonal risks. This becomes the foundation of everything we do for you going forward.

III

Membership begins

Your membership activates. Your point of contact is introduced. Your property enters our care. From this day forward, your home has a professional who knows it — and is accountable for it.

IV

Ongoing stewardship

Scheduled visits, proactive maintenance, vendor coordination, emergency response, monthly reporting. The relationship deepens as we learn your home, your preferences, and how to serve you best over time.

How we hire & vet

Our tradesman
standard is absolute.

The Praesidio vendor network is the product. Every person who enters a member's home has been personally evaluated by our team — not algorithmically matched, not crowd-sourced, not pulled from a directory. We know who they are, we know their work, and we stand behind them.

See How It Works

License verification

Every contractor's license is verified active with the Washington State Department of Labor & Industries before they enter any member property. We recheck annually.

Insurance confirmation

General liability and workers' compensation certificates are on file for every vendor. Coverage amounts are reviewed against the scope of work they perform.

Background checks

All personnel who will be unsupervised in member properties undergo background checks. Our protocol exceeds standard residential screening requirements.

Personal evaluation

We have personally used, evaluated, and worked alongside every vendor in our network. References are checked. Past work is inspected. Our approval is not administrative — it is personal.

Active performance monitoring

We track vendor performance across every job — on-time arrival, scope adherence, quality, and member feedback. Vendors who fall below our standard are removed from the network.

Zero tolerance on conduct

Any vendor who disrespects a member's home, misrepresents their work, or violates our conduct standards is immediately and permanently removed — regardless of their technical skill.

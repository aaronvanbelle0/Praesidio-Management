Three tiers of care

Every home
has a different
story.

Whether you're a full-time resident seeking proactive oversight, or a vacation homeowner who needs year-round stewardship, Praesidio has a membership designed for your exact situation.

Essential Membership
$350 per month

The foundation
of good stewardship.

Essential is designed for full-time residents who want reliable access to vetted tradesmen, a professional eye on their property, and the confidence that when something goes wrong, a trusted resource is only one call away. This is not a reactive service — it's a proactive relationship with your home.

Ideal for

Full-time primary residents in Whatcom or Skagit County who own a home valued at $600K+ and want professional maintenance coordination without a full-time house manager.

Annual Home Assessment

A comprehensive, documented walkthrough of your property conducted by a Praesidio team member each year. We evaluate every major system, identify deferred maintenance, and create a prioritized action plan.

  • Full interior and exterior inspection
  • Roof, gutters, and drainage review
  • HVAC, plumbing, and electrical assessment
  • Written condition report with photos
  • Prioritized maintenance calendar

48-Hour Response Guarantee

When a non-emergency issue arises — a leak, a broken appliance, a fence section down — you call one number and we handle the rest. A Praesidio representative will be on-site within 48 hours to assess and coordinate.

  • Single point of contact, always
  • Initial on-site assessment within 48 hours
  • Vendor sourcing and scheduling
  • Work quality oversight
  • Completion confirmation and reporting

Vetted Tradesman Access

Immediate access to the full Praesidio tradesman network — contractors, plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, landscapers, roofers, and more. Every vendor is licensed, insured, and personally evaluated by our team.

  • 20+ trade categories covered
  • License and insurance verification on file
  • Background-checked professionals only
  • Competitive bid coordination
  • No cold-calling strangers from Yelp

Quarterly Check-In Visits

Four times per year, a Praesidio team member visits your property to perform a walk-around inspection, check seasonal readiness, and log any changes in condition. You receive a brief written summary after each visit.

  • Four scheduled visits per year
  • Exterior and accessible interior review
  • Seasonal readiness check
  • Written visit summary
  • Flagging of any emerging concerns

Project Coordination

Have a repair or improvement in mind? We coordinate the entire project — sourcing bids, vetting contractors, scheduling work, and confirming quality on completion. You don't need to be home.

  • Bid sourcing from vetted contractors
  • Scope of work review
  • Scheduling and access coordination
  • On-site quality check at completion
  • 8% project management fee (on vendor cost)

Seasonal Guidance & Planning

Each season, we send you a tailored home maintenance checklist specific to Northwest Washington's climate — from pre-freeze plumbing prep to summer wildfire defensible space. We can execute any items through our network.

  • Seasonal maintenance checklists (4/year)
  • NW Washington climate-specific guidance
  • Pre-winter systems walkthrough
  • Spring readiness review
  • Optional execution at additional cost
Premier Membership
$750 per month

Full-service care.
Complete peace of mind.

Premier is our most popular membership — the full Praesidio experience. Designed for homeowners who want genuine, hands-off peace of mind: 24-hour on-site emergency response, monthly property reporting, seasonal preparation executed for you, and dedicated contractor oversight. This is the tier where your home truly enters our care.

Ideal for

Busy primary residents, vacation homeowners with frequent absence, and anyone with a property valued at $800K+ who wants active, year-round professional management without the overhead of a full-time employee.

Begin Premier Membership View pricing details

24-Hour Emergency Response

The defining feature of Premier membership. Day or night, weekday or holiday, a Praesidio team member will be on-site within 24 hours of any emergency. We assess, contain, and coordinate remediation — you just need to make one call.

  • 24/7 emergency hotline access
  • On-site within 24 hours, guaranteed
  • Burst pipe, flooding, storm damage response
  • Power failure and security incident coordination
  • Emergency vendor deployment and oversight

Monthly Property Reports

Every month, you receive a professional property condition report: what was inspected, what was found, what was resolved, and what's scheduled next. Full photographic documentation keeps you informed without requiring your presence.

  • 12 detailed written reports per year
  • Photographic documentation of all findings
  • Vendor work completed this month
  • Upcoming scheduled maintenance
  • Year-to-date cost tracking

Full Seasonal Preparation

We don't just hand you a checklist — we execute it. Twice a year (fall and spring), a Praesidio crew prepares your home for the coming season: winterizing, drain-down, system checks, storm prep, and spring re-commissioning.

  • Fall winterization (exterior, irrigation, pipes)
  • Spring re-commissioning and startup
  • Storm preparation before major weather events
  • HVAC filter change and system test
  • Exterior drainage and gutter clearance

Contractor Oversight & Sign-Off

We don't just schedule contractors — we supervise them. A Praesidio manager is present during significant work to ensure scope compliance, quality standards, and property respect. No work is signed off without our approval.

  • On-site presence during major work
  • Scope-of-work compliance review
  • Quality sign-off before final payment
  • Material and product vetting
  • Warranty documentation filing

Emergency Fund Management

Members maintain a reserved emergency fund (minimum $2,500) that Praesidio holds for immediate deployment on urgent repairs. No waiting for wire transfers or card approvals when a pipe bursts at midnight.

  • $2,500 reserve fund held on account
  • Immediate deployment authority for emergencies
  • Full itemized accounting of all expenditures
  • Monthly fund reconciliation
  • Replenishment prompts when balance is low

Vacation Home Arrival Prep

Arriving at your vacation property this weekend? We prepare it for your arrival: heat on, lights on, linens fresh, kitchen stocked with your preferences, exterior inspected. Departure close-down is handled with the same care on your way out.

  • Pre-arrival preparation (heat, lights, linens)
  • Grocery and supply stocking (cost pass-through)
  • Exterior pre-arrival walk
  • Departure close-down and property lock-up
  • Coordinate with cleaning crew
Estate Membership
$1,500 per month · from

A dedicated steward
for your most important
asset.

Estate membership is our most comprehensive offering — a true white-glove partnership. You receive a dedicated house manager assigned personally to your property, same-day emergency response, multi-property coverage, annual capital planning, and access to lifestyle concierge services. Designed for the homeowner who expects their estate to be managed as precisely as their business.

Ideal for

Owners of properties valued at $1.5M+, second and third home owners, estate properties, and anyone who wants a dedicated, named professional managing their home year-round. Also available for multi-property portfolios.

Inquire About Estate Membership

Dedicated House Manager

You are assigned a single named Praesidio professional who serves as your home's dedicated steward. They know your property intimately, maintain your vendor relationships, and are personally reachable by phone or text any time of day.

  • One assigned, named house manager
  • Direct mobile number — always reachable
  • Full property history maintained by one person
  • Vendor relationship management on your behalf
  • Personalized service protocol for your household

Same-Day Emergency Response

Estate members receive our fastest guaranteed response. For any emergency, your dedicated house manager will be on-site the same day — typically within 4–6 hours — with the authority and resources to act immediately.

  • Same-day on-site guarantee (4–6 hour target)
  • Pre-authorized emergency spending up to $10,000
  • Direct escalation to licensed trade professionals
  • Incident documentation and insurance liaison
  • 24/7 availability for your house manager

Multi-Property Coverage

Estate membership covers up to three properties under a single agreement. One manager, one point of contact, one monthly report — covering all of your Northwest Washington residences with the same level of attention each receives individually.

  • Up to 3 properties under one membership
  • Consolidated monthly reporting across all properties
  • Coordinated seasonal prep across all locations
  • Unified vendor network for all properties
  • Additional properties available at reduced rate

Annual Capital Planning & Budget

Each year, your house manager prepares a 5-year capital expenditure forecast for your property — projecting when major systems (roofs, HVAC, windows, decking, irrigation) will reach end-of-life and what they'll cost to replace. No more surprise $40,000 bills. You plan for them years in advance, and we coordinate execution on your timeline and budget.

  • Annual 5-year capital expenditure plan
  • System life expectancy assessments
  • Replacement cost estimates with 2–3 bids
  • Budget timing recommendations
  • Coordination with your accountant or financial advisor
  • Phased execution planning for major improvements

Lifestyle Concierge Services

Estate membership extends beyond the home itself. Your house manager coordinates lifestyle services so your property is ready for exactly how you want to live in it. From arrival provisioning and private chef coordination to security system management, landscape design oversight, and art installation logistics — we handle the details that make your home feel effortlessly managed.

  • Arrival provisioning and household stocking
  • Private chef and catering vendor coordination
  • Security system and smart home management
  • Landscape design and garden crew oversight
  • Art, antique, and specialty item handling logistics
  • Guest and caretaker access management
  • Holiday and seasonal decorating coordination

Estate membership — custom scope available

Estate membership is priced from $1,500/month and scoped individually for each property and portfolio. Larger estates, multi-island portfolios, and properties requiring specialist vendor relationships may be priced accordingly. We invite you to schedule a private consultation so we can design the right scope for your situation.
